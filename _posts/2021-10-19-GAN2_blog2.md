<p>Generative Adversarial Network (<strong>GAN</strong>)  (Goodfellow et al., 2016) is a prevalent method that is wildly used to generate realistic images. However, the generating process of <strong>GAN</strong> is uncontrollable so, to apply <strong>GAN</strong> in the image-to-image translation tasks, <strong>Cycle-GAN</strong>  (Zhu et al., 2017) proposes to regularize the transformation with a <strong>Cycle Consistency Loss</strong>. Compared to other methods, <strong>Cycle-GAN</strong> has following advantages:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Pix2Pix</strong>  (Isola et al., 2017) network requires paired images, that is, highly-related image <span class="katex--inline"><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>x</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">x</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.43056em; vertical-align: 0em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">x</span></span></span></span></span> and image <span class="katex--inline"><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>y</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">y</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.625em; vertical-align: -0.19444em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.03588em;">y</span></span></span></span></span>, to perform translation, which restricts its versatility since the availability of the paired images is limited. Instead, <strong>Cycle-GAN</strong> is more flexible and be able to leverage unpaired images from different domains.</li>
<li>Unlike some <strong>Neural Style Transfer</strong> (Huang and Belongie, 2017) methods that take as inputs two specific images to transfer style from style image to target image, <strong>Cycle-GAN</strong> can capture high-level characteristics of domains and transfer these information between arbitray images from different domains.</li>
<li>The characteristics to be transfered are loosely defined so that we can control the way of transformation by choosing domains.</li>
</ul>
